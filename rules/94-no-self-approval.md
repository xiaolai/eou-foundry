# No Self-Approval Rule

No EOU, skill, script, or agent may be the sole approver of changes to itself.

Generation, auditing, refactoring, approval, and deployment must remain separable.

Generating EOUs may create candidates only. They may not activate, approve, publish, mutate active governance, weaken validators, or rewrite the constitution.
