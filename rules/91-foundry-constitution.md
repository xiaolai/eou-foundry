# Foundry Constitution Rule

Before changing EOU behavior, validation, authority, or promotion rules, read:

- `foundry/constitution.yml`
- `foundry/governance.yml`
- `foundry/failure-taxonomy.yml`

Constitutional invariants cannot be silently weakened. Constitution changes require explicit human review, changelog entry, rollback plan, and cannot be performed by an ordinary EOU.
